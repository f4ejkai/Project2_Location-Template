Santana Row

"The Gathering place for car geeks - R.Wang"

Santana Row Main street

Santana Row Main Street:

For exotic vehicle lovers, Santana Row becomes heaven for car spots on the fourth Sunday of every month. The Cars and Croissants Monthly event is being regularly held here. Only exotics are allowed here, So pack up your camera bag and heads over here. You won't be disappointed.

Tesla Santana Row

Experience Tesla

The only two Tesla dealers/experience stores you have here in Bay Area. Here you could enjoy a demo of the latest technology from Bay Area native auto builder. And you could place your direct order from this place as well. Not a bad place to kill some time.

American Motors Custom and Classics

Travel back in time

Desipite the sign out front has a toyota used cars logo, but that it's as far from what is inside. Lots of classic, almost exclusively American muscle era cars and trucks. Sometimes a little rougher, but always drivers and usually at a super reasonable price. Back to the 60s right from here.

Porsche Stevens Creek

The most exotic dealer in Silicon Valley

Probably the most prestige dealership around Stevens Creek. Occasionally you could see plenty of made-to-order Porsches here. Especially something extraordinary like 918s and Carrera GTs. There is a Porsche Gift Shop there as well; you could come here and grab an excellent gift for your friends.
